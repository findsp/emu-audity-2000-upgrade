# E-mu Audity 2000 Upgrade Repository

## Why this Repo Was Created

Prior to this repository, there was **no official or community-hosted Audity 2000 upgrade project on GitHub**. While firmware upgrades existed historically from E-mu, they were distributed as proprietary OS images, often requiring users to send their units in for service. Hobbyist upgrades and custom ROMs have circulated in forums like Syntaur and Gearspace, but no central, GitHub-accessible source existed.

This repo was created to **archive, preserve, and share the original Audity 2000 OS binaries and the updater tool** for non-commercial use. It provides a single reference point for enthusiasts who want to upgrade their units, follow the documented procedure, and safely access the original OS files without relying on scattered forum posts or unsupported methods.

What *does* exist in the real world regarding upgrading an **E-mu Audity 2000**:

* The Audity 2000 was originally upgraded from **OS v1 to v2** using **firmware updates** supplied by E-mu (officially required sending the unit in for service back in the 1990s/2000s). ([e-UniverCity][2])
