# E-MU Audity 2000 Sound Module

The Audity 2000 is based on an entirely new design which combines:

- 16-channel Rhythmic Pattern Generator / Arpeggiator  
- 12th-order digital resonant modeling filters  
- MIDI clock modulation  
- 24-bit dual stereo effects  
- Four real-time controls  

It includes a variety of dance-type sounds consisting of **noise, analog, and modern synthesizer waveforms**.

---

## Expansion

The **Audity Xtreme** expansion ROM contains 16MB of samples from the **Orbit** and **Planet Phatt** modules with **256 new presets**.

---

## Technical Specifications

- **ROM Size:** 16MB  
- **Sample Rate / Bit Depth:** 39kHz, 16-bit  
- **Presets:** 896 (256 RAM, 640 ROM)  
- **Presets V2:** 1152 (512 RAM, 640 ROM)  
- **Instruments:** 287  
- **SIMM Sockets:** 2  
- **Polyphony:** 32 Voices (64 with V2 upgrade)  
- **MIDI Channels:** 16  
- **Filters:** 50 Z-Plane  
- **FX:** 76  
- **MIDI Ports:** 3  
- **Audio Outs:** 6  
- **Digital Out:** 1 (S/PDIF)

---

## SIMM Specifications

- **SIMM Code Number:** AP530-02  
- **Label:** EXCELSIOR or ISIS  
- **LCD Display Name:** AUDTY  
- **Banks:** 5 Banks

## Factory Demos

This procedure destroys any presets stored in RAM.

1. Hold both Cursor (< >) buttons down and power on the unit.
2. Once booted up into the Diagnostics menu, turn the Data Wheel knob to Initialize NVRAM.
3. Press the Home/Enter button.
4. Press Home/Enter again to confirm.
5. Turn the unit off, wait a few seconds and turn the unit on.
